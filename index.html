About me

Hi! My name is Rio. I am a Fullstack Blockchain developer. With over 5 years of experience in the industry. I have extensive knowledge of various blockchain technologies, including Ethereum and Solana, and have demonstrated my expertise by delivering outstanding projects that showcase my proficiency in building complex NFT applications and smart contracts. One of my most notable works is RetheState, an NFT marketplace that allows users to buy and sell real estate assets as NFTs. I built this platform with in-depth knowledge of NFTs and smart contracts, delivering a scalable and user-friendly platform. In addition to my work on RetheState, I have extensive experience working with decentralized exchanges such as Uniswap, Sushiswap, and Pancake Swap. I have cloned v1, v2, and v3 and created various complex trading sites and crypto exchange applications. I take pride in writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code that adheres to industry best practices. I am proficient in technologies such as TypeScript, Node.js, React, Redux, and CSS-in-JS, which are all essential skills for a Full Stack Blockchain Developer. My skills, experience, and enthusiasm make me a valuable asset to any team. I am passionate about the blockchain industry and am always eager to learn and apply the latest technologies to my work. If you have any questions or would like to discuss a potential collaboration, please don't hesitate to contact me.

I am focused on developing software products , leveraging on blockchain technologies to solve problems within our environment.
